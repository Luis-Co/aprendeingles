Modals "should" & "have to"

Level: A1-A2
Length: 60 min
Topic: Advice vs obligation
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Use should + base verb for advice & opinions
  • Use have to + base verb for external obligation (present)
  • Form negatives & questions correctly (shouldn't, Do you have to...?)

2. Meaning Contrast

External rule / necessity (someone else requires it)
