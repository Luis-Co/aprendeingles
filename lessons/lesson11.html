Prepositions of Place

Level: A1
Length: 60 min
Topic: Locating people & things in space
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Understand and use 12 common place prepositions with correct nouns/pronouns
  • Describe a room, a street map and a picture without pointing
  • Ask and answer "Where is / are…?" naturally
  • Pronounce weak forms & linking: "in the" /ɪn ðə/, "on the" /ɒn ðə/, "next to" /neks tə/
  • Avoid classic L1 interference: in the floor, under the table on the left…

Input & Explanation

1. Core Prepositions of Place

Preposition Meaning Example
in inside a 3-D space The keys are in my bag.
on touching surface There's a cup on the table.
under below / covered The cat is under the bed.
next to / beside at the side He sits next to me.
between in the middle of two The bank is between the café and the school.
behind at the back The garden is behind the house.
in front of facing the front There's a car in front of the door.
above higher than (not touching) The lamp hangs above the table.
over higher covering / across She put a blanket over him.
below lower than Your flat is below mine.
across from / opposite on the other side The pharmacy is across from the bank.

2. Common Phrases

  • on the left / right (NOT in)
  • at the top / bottom of…
  • in the corner (room) vs. on the corner (street intersection)
  • under the bed, on the wall, in the drawer

3. Question Pattern

Where + is/are + subject?
Where is the toilet? – It's behind the stairs.
Where are the keys? – They're on the kitchen table.

4. Pronunciation & Connected Speech

"in the" /ɪn ðə/ → "innuh"
"on the" /ɒn ðə/ → "onnuh"
"next to" /neks tə/ (drop final /t/)
"over the" /ˈəʊ.və ðə/

5. Typical Errors by L1

❌ Wrong ✅ Correct
The phone is in the table. on the table
She is in front the door. in front of the door (needs "of")
He lives opposite to the park. opposite the park (no "to")
The shop is in the corner of the street. on the corner

6. Sequencing for Directions (Preview)

Go straight on, turn left at the traffic lights, the bank is on your right, next to the cinema.
