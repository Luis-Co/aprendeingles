Final Review & Self-Check

Level: A1-A2
Length: 60 min
Topic: Comprehensive review
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Prove A1-A2 competence through mixed-skills quiz (grammar, vocab, functional)
  • Identify personal weak spots & set next-level targets
  • Record a 2-minute fluency video covering 8 core topics
  • Receive self-study roadmap for A2-B1 transition

Part A: Interactive Quiz

1. Grammar Pick (10 items)

1. I ___ to Paris last year.
a) go
b) went
c) goes
2. She ___ a doctor.
a) is
b) are
c) be
3. There ___ any milk in the fridge.
a) isn't
b) aren't
c) is
4. We ___ pizza tonight.
a) are eating
b) eat
c) eats
5. My brother ___ swim when he was five.
a) can
b) could
c) cans
6. This car is ___ than mine.
a) fast
b) faster
c) more fast
7. I ___ my keys. I can't find them.
a) lost
b) have lost
c) lose
8. You ___ wear a seatbelt – it's the law.
a) should
b) have to
c) can
9. It ___ since morning.
a) rains
b) is raining
c) has rained
10. Let's ___ a film tonight.
a) to watch
b) watch
c) watching

2. Functional Gap-Fill (5 items)

a) "___ you like to join us for coffee?" – "I'd love to."
b) "___ about going to the beach tomorrow?"
c) "___ we leave now?"
d) "___ me the bill, please."
e) "___ is the weather like today?"

3. Vocabulary Race (10 items)

1. opposite of "cheap"
2. past of "teach"
3. -ing form of "die"
4. person who brings food
5. you do this with photos
6. comparative of "far"
7. noun: you wear it round your neck
8. adjective: not difficult
9. you listen to it
10. you can "go" it in the mountains

4. Error-Spot Sprint (5 items)

1. She don't like coffee.
2. I have visited Paris yesterday.
3. The weather is rain now.
4. Let's to watch a film.
5. I am not agree.

Part B: Can-Do Checklist

Tick ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌ & note example.

I can… Example I give ✅ / ⚠️ / ❌
introduce myself & others
ask & answer simple present & past questions
describe my town with there is/are & prepositions
order food & handle problems in a restaurant
invite, accept & refuse politely
compare 2 products & recommend the best
talk about future plans (going to & will)
speak about life experiences (Present Perfect ever/never)

Evaluation: < 6 ⚠️ → revise weak lessons; ≥ 6 ✅ → ready for A2-B1 bridge.

Part C: Fluency Mission

Record 2-minute video (phone) including ALL 8 points below (bullet script allowed, no full reading).

1. Name, age, town (present simple)
2. Yesterday – 2 actions (past simple)
3. Tomorrow – 1 plan (going to)
4. Comparison – your town vs another (bigger than…)
5. Experience – Have you ever…? (answer + detail)
6. Invitation – invite viewer to an event next week
7. Weather today – 2 sentences (adjective + verb)
8. Ability – 1 thing you can do & 1 you can't

Tips: short sentences, smile, clear /ɪŋ/ & /θ/ sounds, rising intonation on questions.

Upload: private YouTube or send mp4 → teacher feedback within 48 h.

Part D: Self-Study Road-map A2→B1

Grammar

  • Past Continuous longer actions
  • Used to
  • 1st/2nd conditional
  • Passive voice
  • Reported speech
  • Modals past: should have…

Vocabulary

  • Aim 2,000 words
  • Quizlet "B1 vocabulary" sets
  • Idioms and phrasal verbs
  • Topic-specific vocabulary

Skills

  • Reading: graded readers level 3 (Oxford Bookworms)
  • Listening: BBC "6-Minute English" podcasts 2× week
  • Speaking: 30-min italki / Tandem exchange weekly
  • Writing: keep diary 120 words daily (teacher corrects 1× week)

Exam Options

  • Cambridge A2 Key (KET)
  • B1 Preliminary (PET)
  • IELTS 4.0-5.0
  • TOEFL 40-56
