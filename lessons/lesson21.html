Present Perfect

Level: A1-A2
Length: 60 min
Topic: Life experiences, unfinished past
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Form have/has + past participle correctly
  • Use Present Perfect for life experiences & recent events with present result
  • Place adverbs ever, never, just, already, yet in the right position
  • Ask & answer "Have you ever...?" and "How many times...?"
  • Contrast with Past Simple (time finished vs. unknown/unfinished)

Input & Explanation

1. Form

Affirmative Negative Question
I/You/We/They have ('ve) eaten have not (haven't) eaten Have you eaten?
He/She/It has ('s) eaten has not (hasn't) eaten Has he eaten?

Past participle: regular = -ed (worked), irregular = 3rd column (gone, seen...)

2. Main A1-A2 Uses

Life experiences

(time not mentioned / not finished)

I have visited Paris. (sometime in my life)

Recent events with present result

She has just arrived. (she's here now)

Unfinished time words:

today, this week, ever, never, before

We haven't finished today.

3. Adverb Position

ever / never before past participle

Have you ever eaten sushi? ‚Äì No, I have never eaten sushi.

just / already before participle (UK) or at end (US)

He has just left. / He left just now.

yet = up to now ‚Äì questions & negatives at end

Have they called yet? ‚Äì They haven't called yet.

4. Question Patterns

  • Have you ever...? ‚Üí Yes, I have. / No, I haven't.
  • How many times have you...? ‚Üí Three times. / Never.
  • Where have you been? ‚Üí I've been to London.

5. Past Simple vs. Present Perfect

Past Simple

Finished time: yesterday, last week, in 2015

I went to Paris last year.

Present Perfect

Unfinished / life / recent / no time: ever, never, yet, already, just

I have been to Paris (unspecified).

6. Common Errors

‚ùå Wrong ‚úÖ Correct
I have seen the film yesterday. I saw the film yesterday. (finished time ‚Üí Past Simple)
She has go to Rome. She has gone to Rome. (past participle)
I haven't never been there. I have never been there. / I haven't ever been there.
Have you been ever to Canada? Have you ever been to Canada?
