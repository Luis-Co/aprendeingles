Present Continuous

Level: A1-A2
Length: 60 min
Topic: Actions happening now
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Form affirmative, negative & interrogative present continuous correctly
  • Add -ing with correct spelling rules
  • Contrast Present Simple (habits) vs. Present Continuous (now / temporary)
  • Identify non-action verbs that normally don't take -ing
  • Use time expressions: at the moment, right now, today, this week

Input & Explanation

1. Form Summary

Affirmative Negative Question
I am working am not working Am I working?
He/She/It is working is not (isn't) working Is he working?
You/We/They are working are not (aren't) working Are they working?

Contracted spoken forms: I'm, he's, we're…

2. -ING Spelling Rules

Most verbs

play → playing

Ends with -e

take → taking

(keep → keeping: no drop because two consonants)

Short verb: 1 vowel + 1 consonant

run → running

sit → sitting

Ends with -ie

die → dying

lie → lying

3. Non-Action (Stative) Verbs

These verbs normally don't use continuous form:

like, love, hate, want, need, know, understand, believe, remember, belong, seem, prefer, have (possession), see (opinion)

✅ I like pizza. ✖️I'm liking… (unless slang: "I'm loving it!")

4. Present Simple vs. Present Continuous

Present Simple Present Continuous
Habits / Facts Actions happening now / Temporary
every day, usually, often, never, on Mondays now, at the moment, right now, today, this week
I live in Madrid. (habitual home) I'm staying with friends this week. (temporary)

5. Questions & Short Answers

Are you listening? – Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
Is it raining? – Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

6. Common Errors

❌ Wrong ✅ Correct
He is work now. He is working now.
I am knowing the answer. I know the answer.
They aren't studying English? (word order) Aren't they studying English? (OK informal)
She isn't liking coffee. She doesn't like coffee.
