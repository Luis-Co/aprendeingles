Future with "going to"

Level: A1-A2
Length: 60 min
Topic: Plans, intentions, predictions with evidence
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Use be going to + base verb for pre-decided plans & logical predictions
  • Form affirmative, negative & questions correctly
  • Choose appropriately between going to (plan/evidence) vs. will (instant decision/promise)
  • Apply time expressions: tomorrow, next month, at the weekend, later today
  • Avoid classic errors: I going to, I'm going to goes, I going
