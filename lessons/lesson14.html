Future with "will"

Level: A1-A2
Length: 60 min
Topic: Predictions, promises, instant decisions
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Use will + base verb for predictions, promises, instant decisions & offers
  • Form affirmative, negative & questions correctly (I'll, won't, Will you…?)
  • Reply with short answers: Yes, I will / No, I won't
  • Recognise time markers: tomorrow, next week, in two days, soon, later
  • Avoid classic errors: I will going, she will works

Input & Explanation

1. Form Summary

Affirmative Negative Question
I/You/He/We… will work will not (won't) work Will they work?

NO "to" and NO "-s":
✖️ I will to go. ✖️ She will goes. → ✅ I will go.

2. Short Forms (spoken & informal writing)

I'll / you'll / he'll / she'll / it'll / we'll / they'll
won't = will not (wo + n't irregular spelling)
'll is pronounced /əl/ or /l/ attached to previous word: He'll help /hiːl help/

3. Main Uses of "will" at A1-A2

Prediction

It'll rain tomorrow

(no plan, just opinion)

Instant Decision

I'll buy water

(made at moment of speaking)

Promise / Offer

I'll help you

(willingness to do something)

4. Time Expressions

tomorrow, the day after tomorrow, next week/month/year, in two days, in 2030, soon, later (today), tonight, this weekend

5. Short Answers

Will he come? – Yes, he will. / No, he won't.
( Not: Yes, he'll. )

6. Common Errors

❌ Wrong ✅ Correct
I will going home. I will go home.
She will works here. She will work here.
They will to help us. They will help us.
Yes, I'll. Yes, I will. (short answer)

Time Expressions

today
tomorrow
next week
next month
next year

We use will with all these future time expressions.

Guided Practice

2. Make negative:

you / open / the door / ? →

4. Short answer:

3. Make negative:
