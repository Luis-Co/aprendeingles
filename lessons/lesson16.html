Past Simple: Verb "to be"

Level: A1
Length: 60 min
Topic: was/were, wasn't/weren't, past states & places
Score: 0%

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Use was / were correctly for all persons (I was, they were…)
  • Form negatives wasn't / weren't & questions Was…? / Were…?
  • Answer with short forms: Yes, I was / No, they weren't
  • Apply past-time expressions: yesterday, last night, in 2020, ago
  • Avoid classic errors: I were, he wasn't work, we was

Input & Explanation

1. Form Summary

Affirmative Negative Question
I / He / She / It was was not (wasn't) Was I…?
You / We / They were were not (weren't) Were you…?

NO "did" with "to be" in past:
✖️ Did he was…? → ✅ Was he…?

2. Contractions (spoken & informal)

wasn't /ˈwɒzənt/ weren't /wɜːnt/
Were not = strong denial: We were NOT happy (emphatic)

3. Uses at A1

Past State / Description

She was tired

She was tired. The keys were on the table.

Past Place / Location

I was at home

I was at home last night. They weren't in the photo.

Past Age / Price / Weather

It was sunny

He was 10 in 2015. It was sunny yesterday.

4. Time Expressions

yesterday
last night
last week
in 2020
two days ago

5. Questions & Short Answers

Was the test difficult? – Yes, it was. / No, it wasn't.
Were your parents angry? – Yes, they were. / No, they weren't.

6. Common Errors

❌ Wrong ✅ Correct
I were at school. I was at school.
He wasn't work yesterday. He didn't work OR He wasn't at work.
Was they late? Were they late?
We was happy. We were happy.

Guided Practice

1. Choose was / were:
  1. It cold yesterday.
  2. We at the cinema.
2. Make negative:

She was late. →

3. Make question:

they / at the party / ? →

4. Short answer:

Was he angry? (no) →

2. Negative sentences:
  1. I was at home. →
